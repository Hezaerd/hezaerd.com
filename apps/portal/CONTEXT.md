# Portal

Authenticated Hezaerd surface for internal / product use — not the public Brand or Portfolio sites.

## Language

**Portal**:
The signed-in app for Hezaerd tools and account-backed features.
_Avoid_: Marketing site, Brand, Portfolio, public brochure

**Brand** / **Portfolio**:
Separate public contexts. Portal may link to them; it does not host their content.
_Avoid_: Treating Portal as a second marketing homepage
