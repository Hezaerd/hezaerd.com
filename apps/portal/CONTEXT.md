# Portal

Authenticated dashboard for running the Hezaerd freelance software-selling business — not a public marketing site.

## Language

**Portal**:
The signed-in business dashboard (products, customers, sales, account). Lives at `/`; unauthenticated visitors see a login prompt.
_Avoid_: Landing page, marketing homepage, Brand, Portfolio, public brochure

**Brand** / **Portfolio**:
Separate public contexts. Portal may link to them; it does not host their content.
_Avoid_: Treating Portal as a second marketing homepage
