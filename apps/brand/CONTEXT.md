# Brand

The commercial face of Hezaerd the company: marketing to gather clients, and Work done as the practice for clients.

## Language

**Brand**:
The public commercial site for Hezaerd the company — gathers clients and presents company Work.
_Avoid_: Personal website, portfolio, person archive

**Practice** / **Company**:
The commercial entity under the Hezaerd name — what clients engage.
_Avoid_: Blurring with the person behind the practice

**Work**:
A company/client engagement or commercial outcome shown on Brand. Disjoint from Portfolio Projects — never the same listing.
_Avoid_: Project (Portfolio sense), school project, weekend experiment, full personal archive

**Case Study**:
A deeper Brand-side write-up of a piece of Work for pitching the practice. Lives on Brand, not Portfolio. Not required while Work is scarce — Brand may ship Work as listings first and add Case Study pages when there is enough client Work to pitch.
_Avoid_: Using this name for personal Portfolio Projects; linking Brand Work deep dives into Portfolio; inventing Case Studies to fill empty Work

**Capability**:
What the practice can do for clients — skills and engagement shapes — independent of any single piece of Work. Presented on Brand as **Services** (one surface, not a second nav item). Complements Work when the Work list is thin.
_Avoid_: Fake case studies, invented project themes, padding Work with non-client examples; a separate “Capabilities” nav next to Services
